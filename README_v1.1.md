# Traveller Campaign Brain v1.1

v1.1 establishes a reliable operational model for Garnette and removes all ambiguity between Garnette and Restless Tentacle Syndrome.

## Query Targets

The repository can now answer:

- What kind of ship is Garnette?
- Who owns Garnette?
- Who is authorized to operate it?
- Which rooms exist aboard Garnette?
- What performance and support systems does Garnette have?
- Which published Type-S systems were removed or converted?
- Does Garnette have weapons, an air/raft, probe drones, or a Hiver habitat?
- Where is Tapir?
- Is RTS available?
- Which hidden Garnette features are referee-only or not yet discovered?

## Important Usage Rule

Do not transfer RTS spaces or capabilities to Garnette.

Garnette is a cramped, rebuilt Type-S scout with a good galley, a common room, a multipurpose laboratory, military sensors, a reinforced sensor and communications fit, and a great many scars.

RTS was a separate Hiver transport and is permanently unavailable.
