# Tech World Culture Brain

## Campaign Premise
Tech World was founded by scientists from Neumann who sought distance from Imperial oversight and freedom to experiment with research forbidden or restricted within the Imperium.

## Working Assumptions
- Knowledge restrictions are presumed political until proven otherwise.
- Experimentation is a civic virtue.
- Self-modification may be ordinary.
- Failure is data, not disgrace.
- Personhood may be judged by demonstrated mind rather than biological origin.
- Ownership claims over self-aware constructs are suspect.
- Safety rules are respected when evidence-based, resented when ceremonial.

Do not turn Tech World into a society without ethics. Its blind spots should come from prioritizing discovery, autonomy, and informed consent differently.
