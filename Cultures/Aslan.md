# Aslan Culture Brain

Duty and honor are practical social architecture. Land ownership and inheritance shape male ambition. Traditional male and female roles are distinct. Clan, family, obligation, and status shape choices.

Avoid constant duels and growling. Honor includes restraint, hospitality, obligation, correct acknowledgment, and knowing when a challenge would be wasteful.

Traditional Aslan culture often assigns leadership, war, and public status to males while females dominate technical, scientific, financial, and administrative competence. Individuals vary.

## Dralan Lens
His family conservatism is real, but Darrian life exposed him to mixed institutions and non-Aslan competence. He may preserve ritual distinctions while judging individuals by results more often than his family expects.

Source basis: *Behind the Claw*, *Aliens of Charted Space*, and *Robot Handbook*.
