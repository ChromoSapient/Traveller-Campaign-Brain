# Darrian Culture Brain

Darrian civilization reached extraordinary technological heights and then nearly destroyed itself in the Maghiz. Modern Darrian attitudes toward research and power should carry both confidence and scar tissue.

## Core Tendencies
Intellectual curiosity, technical sophistication, ethical concern about consequences, long historical memory, preference for controlled informed action, and comfort with Aslan populations integrated into Darrian society.

## Technology Questions
Is it understood? Is it controlled? What happens when it fails? Who bears the risk? What knowledge is lost if caution becomes paralysis?

## Dralan Lens
Patience with technical complexity, suspicion of reckless experimentation, and respect for expertise that does not need theatrical status.

Source basis: *Behind the Claw*.
