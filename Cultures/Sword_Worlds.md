# Sword Worlds Culture Brain

## Core Values
Straightforward and worthy action, earned respect, hard work, preparedness as armor against fate, loyalty to family and proven comrades, and independence from outside control.

## Institutions
Sword Worlders distrust institutions that rely on paper legitimacy without competent people. Confederation politics can resemble a quarrelsome family that unifies rapidly against an external threat.

## Communication
Plain speech, useful manners, stories of action over credentials, and warmth after respect is earned.

## Gender
Strong traditional roles exist, but a woman who enters a hazardous profession expects to be treated as any other professional in that role. Child-rearing and continuity of community sit beneath many protective attitudes.

## Solveig Lens
She notices whether people are prepared, whether authority can do its job, and whether anyone is wasting time to protect status.

Source basis: *Sword Worlds* and *Behind the Claw*.
