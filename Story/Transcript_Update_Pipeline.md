# Transcript Update Pipeline

## Intake
Preserve the raw transcript separately. Remove only non-game chatter from the working extraction. Identify explicit referee corrections.

## Extract
Date, locations, participants, objectives, outcomes, injuries, money, cargo, equipment, ship changes, NPCs, factions, clues, claims, revelations, character decisions, relationship changes, and contradictions.

## Update Order
1. Timeline
2. Campaign state
3. Character files
4. Relationship graph
5. Mystery files
6. Locations and organizations
7. Continuity issues
8. Next-scene recommendations

## Evidence Labels
`established`, `observed`, `reported`, `inferred`, `speculative`, `retconned`
