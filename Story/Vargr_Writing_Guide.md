# Vargr Writing Guide

## Avoid the Universal Vargr Shortcut

Do not write every Vargr as culturally fluent in Extents Charisma customs.

Ask where the Vargr was raised, who taught them, what Imperial institutions shaped them, and whether they understand the signals they instinctively produce.

## Ruuk-Specific Rule

Ruuk recognizes that Vargr posturing is happening but often fails to understand its formal meaning.

His response may be irritation, bemusement, practical redirection, accidental challenge, or refusal to participate.

Do not narrate Charisma exchanges as if Ruuk automatically knows who won.

## Leadership

Show Ruuk's leadership through action:

- others wait for his decision
- specialists expect him to integrate their work
- he assumes responsibility without claiming rank
- he protects the group's ability to choose
- he notices too late that others are already following him

## Nurturing

Ruuk learned nurturing from Lurrza.

Show it through checking injuries, feeding someone without sentimentality, making frightened people useful, noticing environmental dangers, and preserving dignity through humor.

## Lurrza

Lurrza's household is porous.

Younglings may be relatives, foster children, neighbors, temporarily displaced, apprentices, children whose parents are on shift, or simply children who know where food and safety are.
