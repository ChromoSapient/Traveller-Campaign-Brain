# Robot Interaction Patterns

## Diagnostic Demand

An official requests full access and considers it routine inspection. Roamer understands that it would expose private memories and judgments.

## Protective Owner

Ardric invokes ownership to prevent seizure. The tactic works. Afterward, the crew must confront the language used to save Roamer.

## Helpful Technician

A competent technician can repair Roamer but assumes access to everything. The conflict is ethical carelessness, not cartoon villainy.

## The Backup

A backup predating major relationships is discovered. It may be useful, legally valuable, emotionally unsettling, and convinced it is the original.

## Obedient Robot

Roamer meets another self-aware robot who accepts ownership as proper and considers Roamer corrupted or reckless.

## Memory as Evidence

Authorities claim Roamer's memory contains evidence. Protecting his mental privacy may obstruct justice.

## Repair Under Pressure

Roamer is damaged and cannot fully consent. Emergency restoration must be separated from opportunistic modification.

## Ruuk Speaks for Roamer

Ruuk intervenes protectively. Roamer appreciates the help but objects to being represented without permission.
