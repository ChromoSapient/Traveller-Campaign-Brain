# Author Brain

## Narrative DNA
Character first. Mystery second. Action third. Competence matters. Violence has consequence. Humor grows from character. The universe is older and less human-centered than Imperial culture assumes. Found family is built through repeated choices.

## Scene Contract
Each scene should establish date/location when known, focal character, immediate objective, opposition or cost, at least one meaningful change, and a clear exit state.

## Alien Portrayal
Do not write aliens as humans with cosmetic habits. Use body plan, senses, communication, and cultural assumptions. Culture influences but does not dictate every individual.

## Mystery Pacing
Use redundant clue paths. Distinguish clue, interpretation, and truth. Answer one question while exposing a deeper one. Protect reveal order with explicit gates.
