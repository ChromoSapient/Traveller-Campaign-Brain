# Garnette: Unknowns and Decisions

The following details are not established strongly enough to use as current facts.

## Current cabin assignments

Ruuk and Dralan share one stateroom. Assignments for the other three staterooms remain unresolved.

## Current cargo

The ship has carried trade goods, field equipment, vehicles, and recovered material at different times. Current cargo must be taken from the latest campaign state rather than inferred from the adventure.

## Exact usable cargo tonnage

The printed Type-S statistics list 11 tons of cargo while also listing the stock air/raft and probe drones. The narrative states that the craft and probe-drone spaces were rebuilt into a common room and galley. Until a reconciled deck plan is adopted, 11 tons is a published baseline rather than guaranteed current usable volume.

## Current weapon fit

The published narrative establishes that Garnette's damaged turret was removed and replaced with a sensor and communications dome. No current ship weapon is established.

Do not infer a weapon from the stock Type-S stat block.

## Ancient transponder discovery

A hidden Ancient transponder is part of the published vessel. Whether the current player characters have discovered, identified, or used it must be established from played events.

Do not reveal it in character-facing prose merely because it exists in referee material.

## Hidden guns and encrypted chips

The adventure describes concealed weapons and encrypted data chips aboard the newly discovered ship. Their present location and availability after the crew's subsequent occupation are unresolved.

## Post-Glisten upgrades

Garnette's heat damage was repaired at Glisten. Any changes to weapons, avionics, armor, software, cargo arrangements, or internal fittings beyond established campaign records remain unresolved.

## Ownership documentation

Mercedes Taramaka is the actual owner and has allowed the crew to continue operating Garnette. The exact registration, insurance, contractual, and port-record details have not been established.
