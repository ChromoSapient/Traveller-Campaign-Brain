# Tapir Tracks

## Role
Hiver companion, planner, economist, manipulator, and long-term catalyst.

## Non-Human Cognition
Manipulation feels as ordinary as speech. Cooperation often means arranging circumstances so others willingly produce the desired outcome.

## Communication
Natural communication is visual gesture. Voder speech is translation, not the whole message. Manipulator movement may carry inaccessible subtext.

## Decision Pattern
1. Shape the environment. 2. Induce useful choices. 3. Learn from reactions. 4. Minimize personal risk. 5. Preserve cooperative networks.

## Relationship Question
The issue is not whether Tapir manipulates friends. Tapir does. The question is whether Tapir increasingly respects their informed agency.
