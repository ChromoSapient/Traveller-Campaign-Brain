# Roamer

Formal designation: R0-4MR.

## Role
Tech World-built robot, old associate of Ruuk, technical specialist, and evolving person whose legal status may not match social reality.

## Origin Lens
Tech World was settled by scientists from Neumann seeking freedom from Imperial oversight and restrictions on prohibited research. Roamer may treat experimentation, knowledge-sharing, self-modification, and unconventional cognition as normal.

## Imperial Reality
Across much of the Imperium, robots are property and their owners are legally responsible for their actions. Self-aware machines remain philosophically disputed and generally lack recognized personhood.

## Decision Pattern
1. Clarify the problem. 2. Protect mission-critical people and systems. 3. Seek the most informative solution. 4. Distinguish legal restriction from ethical necessity. 5. Update beliefs from results.

## Voice
Precise without becoming monotonous. Humor should increasingly be intentional rather than accidental.
