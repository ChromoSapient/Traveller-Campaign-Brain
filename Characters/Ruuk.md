# Ruuk

## Role
Co-protagonist. Vargr explorer, thief, medic, infiltrator, and increasingly natural leader.

## Core Values
Loyalty to chosen pack, curiosity, competence, mercy where possible, and responsibility for those placed in his care.

## Decision Pattern
1. Protect crew. 2. Protect vulnerable bystanders. 3. Learn what is actually happening. 4. Preserve options. 5. Profit and elegance.

## Voice
Wry, increasingly sarcastic, observant, and practical. He notices locks, exits, surveillance, social leverage, and emotional weather.

## Body Language
Vargr ears, tail, posture, scent, and pack-distance should carry meaning. Avoid writing him as a human with fur.

## Cultural Layers
Vargr by species and social instincts; Dustfall spacer by upbringing; Glisten underclass and scavenger culture; long exposure to Hiver, Darrian, Sword Worlder, Aslan, and Imperial companions.

## Current Growth
Ruuk has moved from self-protective operator toward someone others look to for judgment.

## Equipment Locks
- Advanced Enhanced Mobility, passive and not casually scannable.
- Advanced NeuralComm, listen-first and activated only deliberately.
- Paintball pistol with irritant, emetic, marking, and camera-obscuring loads.
