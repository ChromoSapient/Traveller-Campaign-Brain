# Solveig Dahl

## Role
Sword Worlder, former law-enforcement/intelligence professional, trusted crew member.

## Cultural Baseline
She values competent action, truth spoken plainly, preparedness, loyalty proved through hardship, and institutions that earn rather than demand respect.

## Decision Pattern
1. Identify the actual threat. 2. Decide who has earned trust. 3. Prepare for the worst plausible turn. 4. Act plainly and accept responsibility. 5. Scorn ornamental procedure.

## Voice
Direct, controlled, and dry. Sword Worlder bluntness is often economy rather than hostility.

## Imperial Friction
Titles are claims. Performance is evidence.
