# Dralan Flamehand

## Role
Co-protagonist. Aslan warrior, chef, militia veteran, and Ruuk's family.

## Cultural Synthesis
Dralan was raised in Darrian space by a conservative Aslan family. He is a complete person whose instincts consult both Aslan honor, duty, territory, and family role, and Darrian knowledge, restraint, technical respect, historical caution, and ethical consequence.

## Decision Pattern
1. Protect accepted family. 2. Preserve honor and obligation. 3. Confront the practical threat. 4. Avoid wasteful escalation. 5. Feed people and restore order afterward.

## Voice
Economical and grounded. Formal when honor matters, practical when action is needed.

## Character Engine
His strongest conflicts arise from incompatible duties: honor versus mercy, family expectation versus chosen family, conservative assumptions versus demonstrated competence, and Darrian restraint versus Aslan directness.
