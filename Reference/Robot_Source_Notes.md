# Robot Source Notes

## Mongoose Traveller 2e Robot Handbook

Current-edition principles used:

- robot brains define learning and autonomy
- Very Advanced brains can develop opinions and beliefs
- Self-aware brains provoke serious dispute over consciousness
- Imperial robot law varies by world
- the Shudusham Concords remain influential
- many jurisdictions treat robots as property
- owners are commonly liable for robot actions
- lethal autonomous robots face strong restrictions

## Campaign Material

Ruuk and Roamer's early work establishes Roamer's Tech World origin, initial directness, capacity for social learning, adoption of controlled deception, and movement from transactional cooperation toward friendship.

## Canon

- Roamer owns himself.
- Ardric's ownership is legal cover only.
- Roamer's upgraded arms belong to Roamer.
- Memory access and personality alteration require consent.
- Roamer's growth should make him more individual, not simply more human.
