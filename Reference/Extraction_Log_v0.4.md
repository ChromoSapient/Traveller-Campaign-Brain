## Design Decisions

- Lore is edition-aware, not edition-subordinate.
- Mongoose 2e remains the active mechanical default.
- Legacy lore is mined unless contradicted or rejected.
- Every adopted fact should eventually have provenance and dependency records.
- Adventure and magazine material count as important everyday-life sources.
