# Glisten Vargr Source Verification

## Decision

The corsair-remnant origin of the Glisten Vargr population is campaign canon by explicit referee ruling.

## Current Search Result

A targeted search of the currently uploaded *Behind the Claw*, *Mysteries of the Ancients*, and Ruuk campaign material did not locate a definitive published passage establishing the origin.

The absence of a retrieved passage does not invalidate the campaign fact.

## Canon Statement

The Vargr population in the Glisten system descends from remnants or survivors of a pirate or corsair raiding group that ventured deep into Imperial space. Over generations, their descendants became Imperial Vargr, culturally separated from the Vargr Extents and shaped by mixed-species Imperial society.

## Authority

- Source type: explicit referee ruling
- Adoption status: adopted
- Campaign precedence: higher than published setting material
- Published provenance: unresolved and non-blocking

## Future Handling

If a published passage is later found:

1. Record the exact source and page.
2. Compare it with the campaign version.
3. Add compatible dates, names, or circumstances.
4. Preserve the campaign version where details differ unless the referee explicitly retcons it.

Do not return this fact to `unresolved` merely because a source hunt remains incomplete.
