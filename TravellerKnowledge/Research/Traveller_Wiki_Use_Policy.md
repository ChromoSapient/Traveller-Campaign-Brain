# Traveller Wiki Research Policy

The Traveller Wiki is a research index and provenance navigator.

Use it to identify books, alternate names, eras, magazine articles, and page references.

The wiki is not automatically treated as primary canon.

Preferred workflow:

1. Find the relevant wiki entry.
2. Record its cited sources.
3. Retrieve the original source from Dropbox.
4. Extract a concise paraphrase with provenance.
5. Compare editions.
6. Adopt, reject, or leave unresolved.

A wiki claim without a source is a lead, not an adopted fact.

Automated access may be limited by robots policy, so manual links and search snippets may still assist discovery.
