# Sword World Cultural Framework

Common Sword World values include pride, honor, toughness, loyalty, independence, preparedness, and practical competence.

Historical experience encourages contingency planning and suspicion of dependence.

Darrian relations can mix strategic anxiety, resentment, fascination, and reluctant respect.

Solveig should not be reduced to a cultural stereotype. Her useful traits are bluntness, physical confidence, loyalty, direct action, and respect for demonstrated competence.
