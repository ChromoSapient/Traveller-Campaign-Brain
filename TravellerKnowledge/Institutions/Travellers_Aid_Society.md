# Travellers' Aid Society

## Institutional Character

TAS is a respected network of hostels, information services, contacts, and publications.

It is not a secret government.

## Sources of Influence

TAS matters because it provides:

- trusted lodging
- current local information
- introductions
- a members-only social network
- job leads
- archival access
- credibility
- JTAS publication reach

## Membership

Membership may reflect:

- wealth
- service
- social desirability
- sponsorship
- institutional judgment

Not every member is influential, and shared membership does not guarantee cooperation.

## JTAS

The Journal of the Travellers' Aid Society is valuable because it shapes what Travellers believe is happening across Charted Space.

Its influence comes from:

- reach
- reputation
- archive depth
- trusted contributors
- access to local reporting
- ability to frame events

## Campaign Use

TAS can provide access without certainty.

A lead from TAS may be:

- accurate but incomplete
- politically filtered
- reputationally sensitive
- available only to members
- useful because it reveals who is asking questions
