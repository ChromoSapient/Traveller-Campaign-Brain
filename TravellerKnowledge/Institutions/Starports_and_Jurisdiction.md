# Starports and Jurisdiction

## Imperial Territory

A main starport is generally treated as Imperial territory.

Local law usually applies beyond it, especially in the surrounding startown and on the planetary surface.

## Shared Reality

In practice, jurisdiction is often negotiated.

Possible overlaps include:

- customs
- immigration
- weapons control
- quarantine
- criminal investigation
- taxation
- extradition
- docking disputes
- cargo inspection
- security emergencies

## Highport and Downport

A major system may contain:

- highport
- downport
- startown
- local spaceports
- military facilities
- private orbital infrastructure

Different spaces may have different rules, authorities, and enforcement cultures.

## Campaign Use

Starports are ideal pressure points because they combine:

- law
- commerce
- transit
- bureaucracy
- surveillance
- political access
- jurisdictional ambiguity

The crew should rarely be able to assume that "Imperial territory" means simple or uniform law.
