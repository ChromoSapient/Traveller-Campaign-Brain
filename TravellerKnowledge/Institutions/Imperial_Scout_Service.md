# Imperial Interstellar Scout Service

## Institutional Character

The Scouts are decentralized, practical, mobile, and accustomed to incomplete information.

They operate where formal authority is thin and where waiting for instructions may take weeks or months.

## Core Functions

- survey
- exploration
- communications support
- x-boat operations
- mapping
- liaison
- courier work
- frontier contact
- emergency response
- maintaining way stations

## Culture

Common Scout traits include:

- improvisation
- self-reliance
- technical competence
- tolerance for eccentricity
- suspicion of unnecessary formality
- broad local knowledge
- loyalty to mission over appearance

## Institutional Limits

The Scouts are not a universal intelligence service.

They may know routes, rumors, anomalies, and local personalities, but they do not automatically possess authoritative answers.

## Campaign Use

Scouts are useful as:

- credible local contacts
- rumor filters
- route experts
- discoverers of oddities
- reluctant witnesses
- institutional outsiders who can move between worlds

Their power is mobility, continuity, and trust rather than force.
