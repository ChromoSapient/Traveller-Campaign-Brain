# Imperial Navy

## Strategic Role

The Imperial Navy defends the Imperium's interstellar structure.

Its priorities include:

- frontier defense
- deterrence
- protection of major routes
- response to invasion
- suppression of piracy
- support of depots and naval bases
- protection of strategic assets

## Presence

The Navy is powerful but stretched thin.

A system may be nominally protected while seeing little daily naval presence.

Local commanders often act with wide discretion because communications are slow.

## Culture

Common naval values include:

- chain of command
- readiness
- logistics
- discipline
- strategic caution
- procedural legitimacy
- protection of critical infrastructure

## Campaign Use

The Navy should feel:

- formidable when present
- bureaucratic in peacetime
- decisive in crisis
- constrained by jurisdiction and priorities
- alert to Ancient technology, military prototypes, and Omicron-scale threats

A naval officer may care less about a petty crime than whether the crew is carrying something capable of destabilizing a subsector.
