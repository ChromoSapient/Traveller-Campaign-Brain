# Adoption Status

## adopted

True in the campaign unless later retconned.

## compatible

Useful lore with no known contradiction, but not yet required by campaign events.

## alternate

Valid for another edition, region, timeline, or interpretation.

## superseded

Replaced by a higher-authority campaign decision or later source.

## rejected

Explicitly excluded from this campaign.

## unresolved

Conflicting material exists and no interpretation has been selected.

## Usage Rule

Story generation may freely use `adopted` material.

It may use `compatible` material only when doing so does not establish a major new campaign fact.

`alternate`, `superseded`, `rejected`, and `unresolved` material require explicit handling.
